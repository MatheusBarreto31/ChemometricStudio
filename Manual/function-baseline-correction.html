Baseline Correction

Preprocessing

Purpose: Remove systematic baseline shifts common in spectroscopy.

Inputs:
  • X_cal, X_val
Outputs:
  • X_cal, X_val baseline-corrected
Key Parameters:
method
msc, snv, moving_average, or derivative
window_size
Window size for moving average
direction
Axis for multi-way correction
When to Use:
  • When spectra show scatter or baseline drift
  • As an early preprocessing step
  • Before smoothing and scaling
