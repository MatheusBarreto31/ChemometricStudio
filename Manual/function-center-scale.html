Center and Scale

Preprocessing

Purpose: Standardize variables by centering and scaling.

Inputs:
  • X_cal, X_val
Outputs:
  • X_cal, X_val standardized
  • was_scaled indicating applied scaling
Key Parameters:
scaling_method
none, autoscale, or snv
When to Use:
  • When variables have different units or magnitude
  • Before PCA and most regression models
  • Near the end of preprocessing
