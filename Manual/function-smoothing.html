Smoothing

Preprocessing

Purpose: Reduce noise with smoothing filters while preserving signal structure.

Inputs:
  • X_cal, X_val
Outputs:
  • X_cal, X_val smoothed
Key Parameters:
method
savitzky_golay or moving_average
window_size
Smoothing window length
polyorder
Polynomial order for Savitzky-Golay
When to Use:
  • When raw spectra are noisy
  • After baseline correction
  • Before calibration modeling
