Univariate Calibration

Calibration

Purpose: Build polynomial regression models for single-variable prediction.

Inputs:
  • X_cal, Y_cal
  • X_val, Y_val (optional)
Outputs:
  • y_cal_pred, y_val_pred
  • models and metrics
Key Parameters:
degree
Polynomial degree
intercept
Include or omit intercept term
When to Use:
  • For simple and interpretable trend models
  • For diagnostic or baseline calibration
  • For single predictor scenarios
For high-dimensional spectral workflows, use First-Order Calibration instead.
